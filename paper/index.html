Biography

    Qiben Yan is a member of Complex Network & Security Research Laboratory (CNSR Lab), where he is a Ph.D. student since the fall of 2010,under the supervision of Dr. Wenjing Lou. He earned the B.S. degree (2007) and M.S. degree (2010) both from Department of Electrical Engineering, Fudan University, Shanghai, China.

Research Interest

    His current research interests include wireless network security and privacy, network monitoring and forensics, botnet detection, intrusion and anomaly detection, cloud and software-defined networking security. He has also done research in wireless networking, with emphasis on network coding.
